2022年某个夏夜的回忆

Posted on 2024-06-04 Edited on 2024-10-21 In Logs

Preface

Greetings to my readers.

2022 was a confusing year to me.
Honestly, I wasn't very happy and spent a month in solitary.
I should probably attempt to forget that.

It was a normal evening on the way home from school.
With the engine roaring, I looked up at the night sky.
My thoughts were back to the early summer days, just before finals.

To escape from the pressure of studying,
DYT and I had an agreement to meet at the town square at midnight.
We listened to the birds and counted the stars in the night sky,
Writing memories in our own words.

I closed my eyes and felt like I was back in those nostalgic days.

I - 00:28 A.M.

At the time, since we met up at midnight without our parents' permission.
We had to sneak out. The nervousness weighed heavily on our hearts.

We were so caught up in the moment--
--Trying not to get fxxked up.

After all, in our excitement, in the darkest nights,
We failed to record what exactly happended that night.

As a result, now I can only recreate the scenes from my memory which like shit.

To make up for this,
I went out on a couple of clear nights with my phone and recorded some white noise.

II - 01:13 A.M.

DYT and I agreed to meet at 1:15 A.M. at a local mall that's been abandoned.
There were so many buildings and so much chaos that we almost missed each other.

Fortunately, we managed to find each other in the dim light.
Once we were together, we headed to a nearby store for some snacks and drinks.

Then, we walked along the sidewalk for a while.

III - 03:27 A.M.

DYT and I wandered around the town, cursing the school.
And then we came to the square in front of an old city wall.

There were many birds in the trees, flying around and tweeting.

I was so tired that decided to lie on the ground.

As I gazed up at the stars across the night sky,
DYT stood nearby, treating me like I was retarded.

IV - 04:15 A.M.

We came to the entrance of a hospital,
Where there were a lot of shared bicycles parked.

"I don't want to go back."
DYT looks worried.

"We must be killed, so take my last words..."
* weird laughter *

But he went back minutes later.

(Tomorrow, no, we need to go to school at 6 A.M. that morning.)

V - 05:03 A.M.

I walked back to my room, looked at the fan,
Which was quite noisy, and fell asleep right away.

EX - 06:46 A.M.

So the night was over.
Everything went on as usual, as if nothing had happened.

I hope the past exists...

Memories of a Summer Night in 2022
